DoBig

This Discord announcements bot exists for one purpose and one purpose only: to deliver clear, structured, and reliable announcements without distraction, clutter, or off‑topic noise. It is not a chat bot, a moderation tool, a reaction handler, or a general‑purpose utility. It does not respond to commands outside its intended scope, it does not engage in conversation, and it does not perform any tasks unrelated to broadcasting official updates. Its entire design is focused on maintaining a clean, consistent flow of information so that users always know that when this bot speaks, it is delivering something important, relevant, and intentional. By keeping its functionality strictly limited to announcements, the bot ensures that servers remain organized, users stay informed, and the announcement channel retains its clarity and purpose without being diluted by unrelated features or unnecessary interactions.
